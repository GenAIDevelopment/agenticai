# Algebra Notes

- **Definition**: A branch of math using symbols (letters) for numbers/quantities. A "language" for math.
- **Importance**: Building block for STEM. Used in daily life (budgeting, etc.). Develops critical thinking.
- **Key Concepts**:
  - Variables (x, y): unknowns.
  - Constants: fixed numbers.
  - Expressions: combos of v, c, and operators (2x + 5).
  - Equations: expressions with an equals sign (2x + 5 = 15).
- **Example**:
  - 2x + 5 = 15
  - Subtract 5: 2x = 10
  - Divide by 2: x = 5
- **Takeaway**: Powerful tool to solve problems and understand relationships.

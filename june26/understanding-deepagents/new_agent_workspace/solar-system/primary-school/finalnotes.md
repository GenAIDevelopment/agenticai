# The Solar System

## What is the Solar System?
The Solar System is our home in space. It is made up of the Sun and everything that travels around it because of its strong gravity.

## The Sun
The Sun is a giant star at the center of our system. It provides the light and heat that keep us alive on Earth.

## The Planets
There are 8 planets in our Solar System:
1. **Mercury** - The smallest and closest to the Sun.
2. **Venus** - The hottest planet.
3. **Earth** - Our home, the only planet known to have life!
4. **Mars** - Often called the "Red Planet."
5. **Jupiter** - The largest planet.
6. **Saturn** - Known for its beautiful rings.
7. **Uranus** - An ice giant that spins on its side.
8. **Neptune** - The windiest planet, far away from the Sun.

## Other Things in Space
- **Moons:** Many planets have moons that orbit them.
- **Asteroids:** Rocky objects mostly found between Mars and Jupiter.
- **Comets:** Icy balls of dust and rock that glow as they get close to the Sun.

Creating an Account

Customers register using an email address and a secure password. After
registration, a verification email is sent. Verified accounts can place
orders, save addresses, and review previous purchases. This guidance
applies to most online shoe purchases and helps customers understand the
expected process. Example scenario 1 demonstrates a typical customer
interaction.

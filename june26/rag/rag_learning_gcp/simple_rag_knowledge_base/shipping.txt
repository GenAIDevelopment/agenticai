Shipping Services

The store offers standard, express, and same day delivery in selected
cities. Orders are packed within one business day. Customers receive a
tracking number as soon as the courier collects the shipment. This
guidance applies to most online shoe purchases and helps customers
understand the expected process. Example scenario 1 demonstrates a
typical customer interaction.

Tracking

Every order can be tracked from the customer account page. Tracking
information is updated by the courier several times each day. Customers
should allow a few hours after dispatch before the first tracking update
appears. This guidance applies to most online shoe purchases and helps
customers understand the expected process. Example scenario 1
demonstrates a typical customer interaction.

Delivery Issues

Weather conditions, public holidays, customs inspections, and incorrect
addresses can delay shipments. If an order has not arrived after the
estimated delivery date, customer support opens an investigation with
the courier. This guidance applies to most online shoe purchases and
helps customers understand the expected process. Example scenario 1
demonstrates a typical customer interaction.

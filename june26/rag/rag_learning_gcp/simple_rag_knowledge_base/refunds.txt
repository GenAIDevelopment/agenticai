Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within thirty days of receiving their
shoes. The shoes should be in unused condition with the original
packaging. The warehouse team inspects every returned item before
approving the refund. If the shoes show clear signs of use, the refund
request may be rejected. Customers receive an email notification once
the inspection is complete. This guidance applies to most online shoe
purchases and helps customers understand the expected process. Example
scenario 1 demonstrates a typical customer interaction.

Return Process

To start a return, customers sign in to their account, open the order
history, select the purchased item, and submit a return request. After
approval, a return shipping label is generated. Customers should
securely pack the shoes and hand the package to the courier. Once the
warehouse receives the package, the refund process begins. This guidance
applies to most online shoe purchases and helps customers understand the
expected process. Example scenario 1 demonstrates a typical customer
interaction.

Refund Timeline

Credit card refunds normally appear within five to seven business days.
Debit card refunds can take up to ten business days depending on the
bank. Wallet refunds are usually completed within one day. Customers
receive confirmation emails throughout the process. This guidance
applies to most online shoe purchases and helps customers understand the
expected process. Example scenario 1 demonstrates a typical customer
interaction.

Exceptions

Gift cards, shoe cleaning liquids, socks, and customized shoes cannot be
refunded unless they arrive damaged. Promotional discounts are adjusted
automatically if part of an order is returned. If the wrong item is
delivered, the company covers all shipping expenses. This guidance
applies to most online shoe purchases and helps customers understand the
expected process. Example scenario 1 demonstrates a typical customer
interaction.
